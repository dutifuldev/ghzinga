  The 404 is the gateway sending previous_response_id while store=false, and those two are mutually exclusive by     │  
  design. Server-side response chaining only works when the response was persisted, which is exactly what store=false│  
  turns off. So previous_response_id should not be on the request at all when store is false.                        │  
                                                                                                                     │  
  There are two separate defects here and the fix needs both.                                                        │
