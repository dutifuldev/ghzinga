  The 404 is the gateway sending previous_response_id while store=false, and those two are mutually exclusive by design.                                        
  Server-side response chaining only works when the response was persisted, which is exactly what store=false turns off.                                        
  So previous_response_id should not be on the request at all when store is false.                                                                              
                                                                                                                                                                
  There are two separate defects here and the fix needs both.                                                                                                   
                                                                                                                                                                
  The root one is that the request builder is not store-aware. The responses API gives you two modes: chain server-side                                         
  with previous_response_id (requires store=true), or resend the conversation context inline on each turn (works with
